OSupporting DFW Blood Cancer Patients

Our team aims to raise funds that will directly assist blood cancer patients here in the Dallas-Fort Worth area. We want to help local families cover costs for treatment, travel, and other needs.

Accelerating Local Cancer Research

We also strive to advance cancer research at institutions like UT Southwestern Medical Center. Our fundraising will help develop improved therapies and potential cures discovered right here in Texas.

Envisioning a Cancer-Free Community

As students, we have a vision for our community free of blood cancers. Although we cannot accomplish this alone, our campaign gets us one step closer. We aim to raise awareness about these diseases that have affected people we know..

Developing Student Leaders

Participating in this campaign allows ESD students to develop important leadership skills like project management, communication, and teamwork. By working together towards a common goal, we build character and compassion. This experience inspires us to continue making a difference in the future.
