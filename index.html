Our Mission

We aim to support blood cancer patients and fund innovative research by joining forces with the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society. Our team strives to create a future without blood cancers.

Supporting Local Families

Our team strives to provide support for families in the DFW area impacted by blood cancers. We want to help cover treatment costs and other expenses to ease the financial burden.

Research Breakthroughs in Texas

We aim to advance cancer research at institutions in Texas like UT Southwestern. Our fundraising can help develop improved therapies and cures discovered close to home.

Awareness for a Cause

We want to spread awareness about blood cancers that affect those around us. Although we cannot defeat this alone, our campaign gets us one step closer.
